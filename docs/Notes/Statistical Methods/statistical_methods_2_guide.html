Normal Distribution

IQ scores follow \(N(100, 15)\). What percentage of people have IQ between 85 and 115?

Approach 1: Z-Score Method

Z-Score Formula

$$z = \frac{x - \mu}{\sigma}$$
1
Calculate Z-Scores
$$z_1 = \frac{85 - 100}{15} = -1$$ $$z_2 = \frac{115 - 100}{15} = 1$$
2
Use Empirical Rule

Within 1 standard deviation of the mean contains approximately 68% of data

$$P(85 < X < 115) \approx \boxed{68\%}$$
2

Confidence Intervals

Sample of 100 has mean 50 and std dev 10. Find 95% confidence interval for population mean.

Approach 1: Confidence Interval

95% Confidence Interval

$$\bar{x} \pm z_{\alpha/2} \frac{s}{\sqrt{n}}$$ For 95% CI, \(z_{0.025} = 1.96\)
1
Calculate Standard Error
$$SE = \frac{s}{\sqrt{n}} = \frac{10}{\sqrt{100}} = \frac{10}{10} = 1$$
2
Calculate Margin of Error
$$ME = 1.96 \times 1 = 1.96$$
3
Form Confidence Interval
$$50 \pm 1.96 = \boxed{[48.04, 51.96]}$$
